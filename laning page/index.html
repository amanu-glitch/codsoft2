ABOUT AQI VEER

Drive Cleaner. Live Better.

AQI VEER is an AI-powered navigation platform designed to help travelers choose healthier routes based on real-time Air Quality Index (AQI), traffic conditions, and pollution density. Instead of only finding the fastest route, AQI VEER intelligently recommends roads with cleaner air, allowing users to reduce their exposure to harmful pollutants.
